System Features

The Crime Reporting and Management System (CRMS) provides a secure, efficient, and user-friendly platform for citizens and law enforcement to manage crime-related activities digitally.

1. Online Crime Reporting

Citizens can submit crime reports online with location, description, and media uploads such as images, videos, or audio evidence.

2. Case Status Tracking

Users can track the progress of their complaints using a unique Case ID without visiting police stations physically.

3. Evidence Management

Officers can upload, categorize, and manage digital evidence while maintaining chain-of-custody records securely.

4. Officer Dashboard

Provides officers with tools to assign cases, update statuses, add investigation notes, and manage workloads efficiently.

5. Admin Control Panel

Administrators can manage users, roles, crime categories, police stations, and system configurations.

6. Notifications & Alerts

Automatic email and SMS notifications keep citizens informed about case acknowledgements, status updates, and closures.

7. Analytics & Reports

Generates dashboards, trend graphs, and reports to help authorities analyze crime patterns and make data-driven decisions.

8. Secure & Role-Based Access

Ensures data privacy and security through authentication, authorization, and encrypted storage mechanisms.
