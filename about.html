About CRMS

The Crime Reporting and Management System (CRMS) is a web-based application that allows citizens to report crimes online and helps law enforcement officers manage cases efficiently.

This system improves transparency, reduces paperwork, and enables faster communication between citizens and police departments.

Key Features

  • Online Crime Reporting
  • Case Status Tracking
  • Evidence Management
  • Officer & Admin Dashboards
  • Notifications via Email/SMS
  • Secure Data Handling

Project Objective

The objective of CRMS is to provide a secure and user-friendly platform for crime reporting and investigation management, ensuring accountability and efficiency in law enforcement.
